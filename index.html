Our History:

Durham House of Pets was founded in 1990 by Joe Smith. In the last 28 years the company has grown immensely. It started with just a handful of employees and now there are over 200 employees. DHP began with just a few products, and since has expanded to fit all of your pets needs. We began by selling just dog food and cat food, and now we have all types of food, apparel, toys, and accessories for a wide variety of animals.

Why should you use Durham House of Pets?

We are committed to providing you with the best products and services for your pets. We take our job seriously, and want to ensure that every customer is satisfied with the products we provide. We have multiple locations throughout the country to ensure that everyone has access to the best products for their pets. We are competitvely priced and offer many discounts that you won't find at the major chain pet stores. We truly care about our customers, and all of our employees are committed to providing you with the best possible experience.
