Our Policies

Financing:

DHP offers several financing options. We understand that many people do not want to pay the full price up front, so we offer several different options. The options are as follows:

-6 month financing for orders over $300

-12 month financing for orders over $500

-24 month financing for orders over $1,000

Delivery:

Durham House of Pets is committed to making sure your ordering process is easy and timely. We offer free 5 day shipping on orders over $50. Orders that are under $50 will have to pay a shipping fee of $5.95.

Return Policies:

Durham House of Pets accepts returns. If you are not satisfied with your product, you can receive a full refund if the product is unused. This policy only applies during the first 30 days after the purchase. After 30 days the return policy is not longer valid. Always feel free to reach out to our customer service department if you are unsatisfied with a product.
